# Per-biome vegetation + surface-palette readout

Two systems drive what you see on the ground:
- **Ground cover** ‚ÄĒ grasses/ferns/bushes/flowers placed at `surface_y + 1`.
  Defined in [`core/surface_decorator.py:GROUND_COVER_PALETTES`](../core/surface_decorator.py).
  Edit THERE (Python dict, not JSON).
- **Surface block palette** ‚ÄĒ the `grass_block`/`dirt`/`sand`/`podzol` mix UNDER the ground cover.
  Defined in [`config/thresholds.json:noise_layers_biome`](../config/thresholds.json).
  Editable via `tools/world_studio.py` Tab B, or directly in the JSON.

The two systems are independent ‚ÄĒ you can change one without affecting the other.
